ccshare is a CLI tool that allows team members sharing the same Claude subscription to more fairly distribute prompt allocations across both 5-hour and weekly usage windows.

1. 5-HOUR USAGE SMOOTHING

Monitors active prompt volumes in rolling 5-hour slots. If a user exceeds their fair share, ccshare automatically queues requests to guarantee equal access.

2. WEEKLY quota CAPPING

Tracks total tokens spent throughout the week, helping teams stay within boundaries and avoid subscription suspensions or service lockouts.

3. TIERED CAPACITY PROXYING

Optimizes throughput allocations depending on whether you share a Pro ($20/mo), Team ($100/mo), or custom Enterprise ($200/mo) tier subscription.
